About AnimationCord

What is AnimationCord?

AnimationCord is a free, easy, and reliable platform for anyone aged 10–99 who wants to create animations. Whether you’re a beginner or already an experienced animator, AnimationCord helps you bring your ideas to life quickly and creatively.

Features

  • Free: No subscription required — get started instantly.
  • Easy to use: Intuitive interface for beginners and pros alike.
  • Reliable: Safe and fast platform to save and export your animations.
  • Creative Freedom: Multiple tools and effects to make your animations stand out.
  • Accessible: Works on any modern browser.

Why Choose AnimationCord?

We believe animation should be fun and accessible for everyone. No complicated software or steep learning curves. Just log in with your email and start creating your masterpiece.
